# Lenan Bay (Lough Swilly)

## From Rathmullan to Lenan Bay - 20/08/2025

### Crew

- Cyrille Georgel (Solo)

### Narrative

Departed Rathmullan late morning to seek an anchorage closer to the mouth of Lough Swilly, in preparation for passage to Rathlin Island the following day. Found a scenic anchorage at Lenan Bay, well sheltered in the SE wind.
